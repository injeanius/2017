No 1 About Project

Day One Fitness is a direct-to-consumer e-commerce platform for an at-home gym system with patented technology.

The objective of the website is to drive online sales with limited edition fitness equipment sold exclusively through the online store.

No 2 The Process

My role as a front-end designer:

Creating lo-fidelity wireframes, branding, style guides, user journey and final layout / website design. Worked directly with back-end engineers to setup React framework, proprietary e-commerce shopping cart process and coded HTML/CSS from concept to check-out.
